Below I list a selection of of my recent scientific publications that are accepted for publication in peer reviewed journals. You can find a full list of my publications on [ADS](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/public-libraries/IL-k-aMpRAGkjTzwtpXONA).

1. Magnetic field alignment with dense cores in the transition between cloud and core scales, S. Yin, **A. Pandhi**, et al., 2025, ApJ, in press.

2. Evolution of polarization properties of 28 repeating FRBs with multi-year CHIME/FRB observations, C. Ng, **A. Pandhi**, et al., 2025, ApJ, 982, 2 [ADS](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2025ApJ...982..154N/abstract).

3. Improved constraints on the magnetoionic environment of eight fast radio bursts using dense grids of polarized radio galaxies, **A. Pandhi** et al., 2025, ApJ, 982, 2, [ADS](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2025ApJ...982..146P/abstract).

4. Choosing interpolation techniques for reconstructing Galactic Faraday rotation, A. Khadir, **A. Pandhi** et al., 2024, ApJ, 977, 2, [ADS](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2024ApJ...977..276K/abstract).

5. Polarization properties of the 128 apparently non-repeating fast radio bursts from the first CHIME/FRB baseband catalog, **A. Pandhi** et al., 2024, ApJ, 968, 2, [ADS](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2024ApJ...968...50P/abstract).

6. Alignment of dense molecular core morphology and velocity gradients with ambient magnetic fields, **A. Pandhi** et al., 2023, MNRAS, 525, 1, [ADS](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2023MNRAS.525..364P/abstract).

7. A method for reconstructing the Galactic magnetic field using dispersion of fast radio bursts and Faraday rotation of radio galaxies, **A. Pandhi**, S. Hutschenreuter, J. L. West, B. M. Gaensler, A. Stock, 2022, MNRAS 516, 4, [ADS](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2022MNRAS.516.4739P/abstract).

8. Faraday rotation measures of Northern hemisphere pulsars using CHIME/Pulsar, C. Ng, **A. Pandhi** et al., 2020, MNRAS 496, 3, [ADS](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2020MNRAS.496.2836N/abstract).
